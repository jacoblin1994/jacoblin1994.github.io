Why This Blog

Dec 16 2021

Why do I care?#

One thing I realized after college is that life is a learning journey that never ends. Given the rapidly changing world, it is almost always the case that what you learn in the traditional four-year college program cannot suffice the constantly changing needs forever.

In fact, it might quite be the contrary that what you are taught in school is rarely used. This is not to discredit the importance of education but rather it is the learning process that really counts because the knowledge could itself become outdated yet the process by which you learn is here to stay and get you ‘sync’d’ or relevant.

Everyone have their own ways to learn. For me, I become demotivated easily. Therefore, I wanted to establish my own learning method based on the Golden Circle model that could not only inspire me to learn but also hold me accountable for it.

How to do it?#

I will use this blog to document all the Why, How, and What of my learning journey to keep myself motivated and accountable. This will also drastically mitigate my troubling memory. T.T
