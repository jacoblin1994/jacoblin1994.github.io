What is it?#

The Golden Circle is a model developed by Simon Sinek in his book, Start with Why, which talks about the concept that

people are inspired by a sense of purpose (or “Why”), and that this should come first when communicating, before “How” and “What”

And the scale of which could range from individuals to organizations.
